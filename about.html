Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Victor Olamide, THE VICTOR BIKES started as a small bicycle repair shop in Abuja. What began as a passion for cycling and mechanics has grown into one of Nigeria's most trusted bicycle stores.

Victor, an avid cyclist himself, noticed the lack of quality bicycle services and genuine parts in Nigeria. He started by fixing neighbors' bikes from his garage, and through word-of-mouth recommendations, the business grew rapidly.

Today, we operate from our main store at Palm City Street, Abuja, serving customers from all over Nigeria. We've expanded from basic repairs to selling a wide range of bicycles, electric vehicles, parts, and accessories.

"Our mission is simple: to promote healthy living through cycling while providing top-quality products and services at affordable prices. We believe everyone deserves access to reliable transportation and the joy of riding." - Victor Olamide, Founder

We offer a wide range of bicycles from mountain bikes and road bikes to kids' bicycles and electric bikes. All our bikes come with warranty and after-sales support.

Our certified technicians provide professional repair services for all types of bicycles, from basic tune-ups to complete overhauls and electric bike servicing.

We stock genuine parts from leading brands like Shimano, Continental, and our own Victor brand. From brakes and gears to helmets and locks, we have it all.
